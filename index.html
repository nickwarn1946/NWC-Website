About Nick

Experience You Can

NW
Certified Management Consultant Chartered Engineer Fellow, UK IoC Chair, ICMCI

Nicholas Warn (Nick) is a Chartered Engineer and Certified Management Consultant with over three decades of experience helping organisations transform and perform. He is a Fellow of the UK Institute of Consulting, and currently serves as Chair of the International Council of Management Consulting Institutes (ICMCI) — the global body for the consulting profession — where he also represents the ICMCI Board on its accreditation body, the Institute Quality Assurance (IQA).

In that role, Nick has been instrumental in developing and publishing the global competence framework for individual consultants, the Code of Professional Conduct, and the implementation framework for applying the ISO 20700 Guidance Standard for management consultancy services.

Nick founded his consultancy practice in 1990, working across multiple sectors in business transformation and performance improvement, with international collaborations in Eastern Europe and the Middle East. Before entering consultancy, he built a successful industrial career with Dunlop across the UK, India and Germany, before becoming a Director of companies in the packaging and security printing sectors.

Alongside his consulting work, Nick is a Director of a Hampshire-based charity providing furniture and household essentials for people in need, and offering training and work experience to volunteers entering employment. He is married with two grown-up children and four grandsons — and when he's not at the strategy table, you'll find him on the golf course or in the vegetable garden.

The Model

Dragonfly Strategy

"Three hundred million years of evolutionary refinement. One creature that never went extinct. Not through size or strength — through intelligent agility."

The world has changed. Traditional strategy hasn't kept up. Built for slower, more predictable environments, conventional planning frameworks actively mislead when volatility becomes the norm.

Dragonfly Strategy is a new and innovative consulting model — forged for turbulence. It translates the dragonfly's extraordinary biological capabilities into a practical leadership system: one that helps organisations see more clearly, move more intelligently, focus more purposefully, and collaborate more effectively.
